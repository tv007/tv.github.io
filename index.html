Pittsburgh Sleep Quality Index (PSQI)

Question Not during the past month (0) Less than once a week (1) Once or twice a week (2) Three or more times a week (3)
1. During the past month, how would you rate your sleep quality overall?
2. During the past month, how often have you had trouble sleeping because you cannot get to sleep within 30 minutes?
3. During the past month, how often have you had trouble sleeping because you wake up in the middle of the night or early morning?
4. During the past month, how often have you had trouble sleeping because you have to get up to use the bathroom?
5. During the past month, how often have you had trouble sleeping because you cannot breathe comfortably?
6. During the past month, how often have you had trouble sleeping because you cough or snore loudly?
7. During the past month, how often have you had trouble sleeping because you feel too cold?
8. During the past month, how often have you had trouble sleeping because you feel too hot?
9. During the past month, how often have you had trouble sleeping because you have bad dreams?
10. During the past month, how often have you had trouble sleeping because you have pain?
11. During the past month, how would you rate the quality of your sleep overall?
